About

The artist, the comics, and how this thing came to be.

Who made this?

Hey — I'm the artist behind Stay Tooned GFX. I make comics that wander freely and laugh strangely. This site is hand-built from scratch to be exactly what a webcomic site should be: no ads, no algorithm, no nonsense — just the work.

More about me goes here. Where you're from, what drives the work, how long you've been drawing, what you do outside of comics. Keep it personal.

The Series

It's a Good Life
A newspaper-strip style comic about the absurd little moments that make up every day. Dry, warm, and frequently bewildered.
Melvin
The misadventures of Melvin — everyone's favorite disaster. Full-page story comics about being a mess and somehow surviving it.
Dio La Damned
Dark, dramatic, and a little bit damned. Dio's world is not what it seems. Horror-adjacent graphic novel told in chapters.

Tools

Comics are drawn digitally in Clip Studio Paint, lettered by hand, and exported as WebP for the web. This site is hand-coded HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no CMS. It lives on GitHub Pages.
